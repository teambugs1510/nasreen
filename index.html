NowLive is India's first LiveShopping Entertainment Company, owned by Networkbay Retail. Our ambition is to provide exciting Live Shopping experiences for shoppers of all ages.

We partner with brands and creators to create LiveShopping experiences which delight shoppers.

FAQS

Live Shopping is the ability to shop and buy a product while watching a live stream. No longer is it needed to see a video, and then go to the website. While seeing a live shopping experience, you could immediately click on the buy button in the live stream and buy the product. Or chat with your host and give reactions.

Every live show has a chat box at the bottom of the show. You can use this to communicate to the host and give feedback. Of course, due to traffic the host may not be able to reply back immediately but our team will review and try our best to respond.

An interactive video allows the viewer to click on different hotspots on the video and control how they see the video. They can click on different buttons and action items and discover the product for themselves.
